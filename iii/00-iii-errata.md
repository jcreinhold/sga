# Errata — Tome III

<!-- label: III.errata-iii -->

Misprints and errors in the new edition of SGA 3, Tome 3.

*13 October 2024.*

## 1. SGA 3, Tome 3

### 1.1. Exposé XIX

- p. 15, line 4: after "take `T` split", add: "and use the proof of Th. 2.5".
- p. 23, line −4 of 5.13: `t ≠ 0` should be `b ≠ 0`.
- p. 24, at the level of page 33 of the original: add "of `r` copies" before "of `n G =`". And, 6 lines below, replace
    `n G_{m,S′}` by `(n G_{m,S′})^r`.

### 1.2. Exposé XX

- p. 29, 2 lines below original page 39: `x` should be `X`.

- p. 37, 3 lines above B), `G(0) = 1` should be `E(0) = 1`.

- p. 38, line above C), `r` should be `α`.

- p. 38, 2 lines below C), `N(S′)` should be `N′(S′)`.

- p. 40, 1st formula in D): `E(x)` should be `E(x)⁻¹`. (This formula was not written in the original.)

- p. 40, paragraph E): in the 3 first formulas with `α(H)` on the left, replace on the right `(1 + bx)` by `(1 + ax)`.
    The proof is unchanged.

- p. 45, Th. 3.1 (v): the 1st formula is incorrect and `Y` is undefined. (This error appears in the original.) One
    should understand that `Y = zX`, with `z ∈ G_m(S′)`; then by (iv) one has `w_α(Y) = w_α(X) α^∨(z⁻¹)`, hence
    `w_α(X)² = α^∨(−1)` gives

    ```text
    w_α(X) w_α(Y) = α^∨(−z⁻¹).
    ```

    Finally, `X⁻¹` and `Y⁻¹` are defined in Corollary 2.6: one has `XX⁻¹ = 1` and `Y⁻¹ = z⁻¹ X⁻¹`, hence indeed
    `−z⁻¹ = −X Y⁻¹`.

- p. 51, Prop. 3.12: replace `Q(S)` by `N^×(S)`.

### 1.3. Exposé XXI

- p. 85, N.D.E. (19): a closing parenthesis is missing after `w_{k−1}(ρ)`.

### 1.4. Exposé XXII

- p. 122, last line of 4.1.12: `exp_α(Y)` should be `exp_{−α}(Y)`.

### 1.5. Exposé XXIII

- p. 210, line above 6.2: replace `−X_{−α}⁻¹` by `−X_α⁻¹`.

### 1.6. Exposé XXIV

- p. 245, line 8 of the proof of 4.5.1: `o∼` should be `où`.

### 1.7. Exposé XXVI

- p. 311, diagram of 4.5.3: extra parenthesis before the last `O_f`.
